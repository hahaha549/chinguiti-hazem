How I Work

My process.

Four steps. Always in order. Skipping any one of them creates problems later.

1
Step 01
Understand the Problem

Talk to people. Ask the uncomfortable questions. Find the gap between what users say they want and what they actually need.

2
Step 02
Structure the Solution

Turn insight into architecture. Map the flows, define hierarchy, remove everything that doesn't earn its place.

3
Step 03
Design Clean Interface

Visuals that serve function. Strong spacing, clear type, honest color. No decoration for its own sake.

4
Step 04
Build and Refine

Write clean, structured code. Test with real users. Iterate until it actually works — not until it looks like it works.
